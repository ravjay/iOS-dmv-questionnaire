• Complete a Driver License or Identification Card Application form (DL 44). To obtain this form by mail, call DMV's automated phone service 24 hours a day, 7 days a week at 1-800-777-0133.
  • Present an acceptable Identity document.
  • Present an acceptable residency document.
  • Provide your social security number.
    Note: If you are applying for a REAL ID compliant Identification card, you must provide proof of your SSN. 
  • Provide your true full name.
  • Pay the application fee. There is no charge for a Senior Citizen ID card. You must be age 62 or older for a Senior Citizen ID card.
